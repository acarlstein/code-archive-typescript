Patterns: Behavioral: Chain of Responsibility

In Construction - Ignore this page

Definition
The chain of responsabilities has a source of command objects and a series of processing objects.

  • The command objects encapsulate all information needed.
  • The processing objects contains the logic.

Usages

  • When you wish to prevent coupling between sender and receiver.
  • When you wish to chain the receiving objects and pass the request along a chain until an object handle it.
  • When you wish to have a single processing pipeline that take care of launch-and leave requests with many possible handlers.
